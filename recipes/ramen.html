RAMEN

Description

Below you'll find step by step instructions on how to make a delicious Japanese style ramen, with simple ingredients right from your home!

Don't forget to let us know how our recipe turned out for you! Share a picture with us on Twitter or Instagram!

Ingredients:

  • Pork Broth
  • 1 egg
  • Scallions
  • Wheat Based Noodles
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • Chili Oil

STEPS:

  1. Boil pork broth in deep pan to medium heat
  2. Once boil starts, add rest of ingredients except egg!
  3. continue to boil until noodles are cooked!
  4. boil egg separately in smaller pan
  5. Once egg is boiled, peel and cut in half
  6. Serve ramen with egg!
